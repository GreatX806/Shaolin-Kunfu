Our Story

A Legacy of
Authentic Taste

Shaolin Kungfu Chinese Restaurant brings the soul of authentic Chinese cuisine to the heart of Lekki. Nestled in the prestigious NICON Estate area along Lekki–Epe Expressway, we have established ourselves as Lagos's premier destination for genuine Chinese flavours — from classic noodle and rice dishes to bold, flavourful entrees crafted for both local and international palates.

Our kitchen blends rich, time-honoured recipes with the finest fresh ingredients, delivering an experience that is at once authentic and unforgettable. Whether you're joining us for a family feast, a romantic dinner, or a celebratory night out, we promise an evening of culinary excellence wrapped in a warm, luxurious atmosphere.
