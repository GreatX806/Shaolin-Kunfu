Our Story

Where Tradition Meets
Lagos Luxury

Nestled in the vibrant NICON Estate along Lekki–Epe Expressway, Shaolin Kungfu Chinese Restaurant is Lagos's premier destination for authentic Chinese cuisine. We bring the bold, time-honoured flavours of China to the tables of Lekki — crafted with the freshest ingredients and decades of culinary mastery.

From our sizzling grill station to our signature cocktail bar, every detail has been thoughtfully curated for an experience that transcends the ordinary.

  • Authentic Chinese wok-cooking techniques passed through generations
  • Premium ingredients sourced to honour every dish's origins
  • A dining space designed for intimacy, celebration, and discovery
